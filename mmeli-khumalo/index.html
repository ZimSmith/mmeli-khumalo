About Mmeli

Mmeli Khumalo is a kasi boy from Johannesburg who rose from the streets to television screens, modelling campaigns, and digital platforms. His journey embodies resilience, creativity, and authenticity — showing that something real can rise from nothing.

Through Broken Projects, he channels this lived experience into a restorative movement that uplifts people and places, proving that township excellence is not only possible, but powerful.
